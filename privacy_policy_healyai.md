## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways when you use the App. The information we may collect includes:

### A. Personal Data
When you register for an account (via email or Google Sign-In), we may collect personally identifiable information, such as:
*   **Identity Data:** Your full name and profile picture.
*   **Contact Data:** Your email address.

### B. Health and Medical Information
To provide you with personalized AI analysis, you may voluntarily submit sensitive health information, including:
*   **Symptom Data:** Information regarding physical or mental symptoms, their severity, and duration.
*   **Patient Profiles:** Information indicating whether the query is for yourself, a pediatric patient, or a geriatric patient.
*   **Medical Documents:** PDF documents and images you upload, which may include insurance plans, medical bills, prescription labels, or lab reports.

### C. Device Permissions and Media
To utilize certain features, the App requires access to your device's hardware:
*   **Camera & Photo Library:** To allow you to take and upload photos of documents, bills, or physical conditions for AI analysis.
*   **Microphone:** To enable Voice-to-Text interactions with the AI chat system.
*   **Files & Storage:** To allow you to upload PDF documents from your device storage.

### D. Automatically Collected Data
We may automatically collect non-identifiable usage information, such as your device type, operating system version, app interaction logs, and crash reports to improve our services.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the App to:
*   Create and manage your user account.
*   Provide AI-driven insights regarding your symptoms, medical documents, and insurance inquiries.
*   Process voice inputs into text to facilitate hands-free chat interactions.
*   Deliver text-to-speech audio responses.
*   Improve the accuracy and responsiveness of the App's AI algorithms.
*   Provide customer support and troubleshoot application errors.
